Welcome!
My name is Leo (he/him/any). I am an aspiring astronomer and educator from North Macedonia. I got my bachelor's summa cum laude in Physics and Math from Tufts University (Medford, MA) in 2025 and I am an incoming graduate student at Stanford University (Stanford, CA) pursuing my PhD in Physics.
My main research interest is in the formation of stuctures in the Universe, specifically the formation and growth of dark matter halos and how this connects to the formation and evolution of galaxies. This is often referred to as the galaxy-halo connection.
I am passionate about education and science outreach. My hobbies include running, collecting vintage books, and doing crosswords.
